About

An interactive 3D web application that visualises the quantum double-slit experiment.

Users fire photons or electrons at a barrier with two slits and watch an interference pattern build on a detection screen. Four features combine to create a unique educational tool that no competitor offers together.

Features

01

Timeline Scrubber

Drag through time to watch particles accumulate dot by dot, or rewind to see fewer. All trajectories pre-computed; the timeline controls visibility.

02

Observer Toggle

When "observing," the interference pattern collapses to two classical bands. Toggle off and the wave pattern returns. The "wow" moment — students see wave function collapse in real time.

03

360° Orbital Camera

Full OrbitControls: rotate, zoom, pan around the experiment like a physics bench. Keyboard alternatives for every mouse action.

04

Adjustable Parameters

Slit width, slit separation, wavelength (changes particle colour), emission rate, distance to screen. Real-time tweaking.
