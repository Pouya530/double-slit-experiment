How this demo works

These controls mirror the main product: timeline, measurement strength, camera, and experiment parameters.

  • Timeline — Drag the slider to scrub through time, or use Play / Pause (in the bar or inside Explore deeper) to run or hold the animation.
  • Explore deeper — Drag γ from wave-like (0%) toward full which-path (100%). The pattern updates with Englert complementarity (V = √(1−γ²)). A ◎ badge appears at 100%. The small diagram shows (D, V) with D = γ.
  • Camera — Drag to orbit, scroll or pinch to zoom, right-drag (or trackpad two-finger) to pan—full 3D around the bench.
  • Parameters & Recompute — Slit width, separation, and wavelength (colour). After edits, Recompute regenerates the particle set. Extended options in this build add mass-related behaviour for some interpretations.
  • Interpretations & ℹ — Tabs switch the story and overlays (Copenhagen, pilot-wave, etc.). The ℹ panel explains the active view without changing the underlying simulation.
  • Theme — Use the sun/moon control in the header for light or dark UI.
  • Focus view — Use Focus in the header for a clean canvas. Interpretation (tabs and ℹ) stay at the top; Parameters and Explore deeper live in the Controls side sheet. Close the sheet with ×, the dimmed area, or Esc (twice to exit focus). The timeline stays usable at the bottom.

Tour — On the classic demo, use ▶ Tour next to ℹ for a guided walkthrough.
